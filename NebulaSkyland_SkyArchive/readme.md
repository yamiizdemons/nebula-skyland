# ?? Nebula Skyland

A floating world of sky paths, drifting islands, living memories, and pterosaur-born souls.

Nebula Skyland is not a fixed place. It evolves through its inhabitants, emotions, and the shifting sky currents that connect everything.

---

## ?? What is Nebula Skyland?

Nebula Skyland is an airborne ecosystem made of:
- Floating landmasses
- Connected sky routes
- Ancient hybrid pterosaur species
- Healing sanctuaries and academies
- Emotional and biological systems intertwined with the sky itself

It is a living world, constantly shifting rather than remaining static.

---

## ??? Major Locations

### ??? Sky Paths
Aerial routes that connect all regions of Nebula Skyland.  
They are constantly changing due to wind currents and emotional disturbances.

### ?? Nebula Skyland Academy
A learning and survival institution for skyborn beings.  
Focuses on flight mastery, ecology, and adaptation.

### ?? Sakura Mansion
A healing residence built in traditional Shoin-zukuri style.  
A sanctuary for recovery, rest, and emotional stabilization.

### ?? Floating Islands
Various habitats suspended in the sky, each with unique ecosystems and histories.
