---

🖤 Core Theme

Nebula Skyland is a fictional world centered around intelligent pterosaurs living among floating islands, celestial skies, forests, mountains, and ancient landscapes.

The world blends:

✦ Paleontology

✦ Fantasy-inspired scenery

✦ Slice-of-life adventures

✦ Comedy

✦ Family bonds

✦ Mystery

✦ Emotional storytelling

---

☾ Visual Direction

Gothic × Celestial × Natural

Visual inspirations include:

* Moonlit skies
* Nebulas
* Stars
* Floating islands
* Ancient ruins
* Gothic ornamentation
* Dusty pastel colors
* Elegant silhouettes
* Soft glowing accents

The world should feel beautiful rather than frightening.

---

🦴 Biology

All major characters are based on real pterosaur groups.

Examples include:

• Azhdarchids

• Anhanguerids

• Nyctosaurids

• Rhamphorhynchids

Characters remain true pterosaurs while featuring fictional ornamental traits.

---

🖤 Kuroshiko

An original ornamental azhdarchid lineage.

Common features:

• Nyx-colored skin

• Dusty-colored crests

• Gothic, Yamikawaii, or Jirai-inspired markings

• Elegant anatomy

• Decorative wing patterns

• Natural pigmentation

Kuroshiko are always true pterosaurs.

---

🩷 Shinzōshiko

A peaceful sister lineage to the Kuroshiko.

Traits include:

• Gentle temperament

• Fish specialist

• Softer ornamentation

• Graceful crest shapes

• Elegant aquatic hunting

🌌 Tone

Nebula Skyland balances several moods:

☽ Cozy

☽ Whimsical

☽ Chaotic comedy

☽ Emotional moments

☽ Quiet exploration

☽ Heartwarming friendships

Even during serious moments, hope remains a central theme.

---

✦ Character Philosophy

Every character should have:

• A distinct silhouette

• A recognizable color palette

• A unique personality

• Natural-looking anatomy

• Individual habits and quirks

No two characters should feel visually interchangeable.

---

🕯️ Design Language

Recurring motifs include:

♡ Hearts

✦ Stars

☾ Crescent moons

❖ Gothic lace

🕊️ Elegant linework

🪽 Ornamental crests

🌸 Dusty colors

🌌 Nebula-inspired gradients

These motifs are integrated into the characters' natural anatomy rather than worn as accessories.

---

☁ World Identity

Nebula Skyland is not about humans.

It is a world where the skies belong to pterosaurs, where friendships soar above the clouds, and where ancient creatures leave graceful silhouettes beneath constellations that never fade.

"Every wingbeat carries another story across the endless sky."
