# 🌌 Nebula Skyland 🪽

A floating sky-world where ancient wings carve constellations into the clouds and emotions ripple through the wind like living weather.

## 🌠 Overview

Nebula Skyland is a high-altitude fantasy ecosystem built around sentient pterosaur-like beings living across drifting sky-islands connected by luminous aerial pathways.

Here, memory becomes geography, friendships reshape weather patterns, and entire regions of the sky respond to emotional energy.

## 🪽 World Structure

- Nebula Skyland Paths — glowing aerial routes linking homes, schools, and sacred locations  
- Nebula Skyland Academy — the central learning hub where young flyers train, study, and bond  
- Sakura Mansion — a traditional Shoin-zukuri healing residence where injured or lost beings are cared for  
- Floating districts, cloud forests, and starlit migration zones scattered across the sky layers  

## 🌑 Kuroshiko Family

- Yamidrakon Kuroshiko (Yami) — chaotic, instinct-driven protagonist with deep emotional layers, sudden energetic spirals, and reflective pauses  
- Nyxie — calm Quetzalcoatlus leader, older sibling figure, observant and grounded  
- Melody — gentle Arambourgiana, soft-hearted and playful, emotional warmth of the group  
- Momoka (Moe) — comforting healer cousin, shy, soothing, and emotionally supportive  

## 🌠 Academy & Sky Residents

- Ryuu (Hatzegopteryx) — stoic, fish-loving presence with quiet intensity  
- Akira (Nyctosaurus, Sakura Variant) — tall, elegant healer with a short crest and rare sakura traits  
- Sirius (Nemicolopterus) — energetic “shooting star” assistant bursting with movement and light  
- Haruka — soft pink sky flyer with gentle emotional presence  
- Mizuki — pale sky-blue flyer with soft luminous accents  
- Naoki — navy-toned celestial flyer with moon-and-star markings  
- Kazuki — cosmic-toned flyer with galaxy patterns and glowing accents  
- Sora — snow-white flyer with sky-blue crystalline patterns  

## 🌸 Sakura Mansion Staff

- Akira — main healer, calm and precise under pressure  
- Sakura — secondary healer, steady and supportive  
- Momo — blossom-themed caretaker, gentle and nurturing  
- Ayuka — protective Eurazhdarcho healer with strong defensive instincts  

## ✨ Stargazer Line

- Hoshiko — calm cartographer and astronomer, mapping sky currents and celestial drift patterns  
- Naoki — connected to celestial navigation and star-guided flight paths  

## 💫 Canon Core

- Emotional energy directly influences the environment of Nebula Skyland  
- Healing, memory, and recovery are central world mechanics  
- The sky behaves like a responsive living ecosystem  
- Ryukira (Ryuu × Akira) is the primary canon pairing  

## 🌈 Tone & Themes

- Floating sky fantasy with emotional depth  
- Found-family dynamics across species and personalities  
- Healing after chaos and trauma  
- Exploration of memory, identity, and connection  
- Soft cosmic wonder mixed with chaotic bursts of character energy  

## 🪶 Core Idea

Wings are not just for flight.

In Nebula Skyland, they are for remembering, connecting, and surviving the weight of the sky itself.
